  • Safety from the Ground Up

    We’re going above and beyond to keep our customers and crewmembers safe and well. Our multi-layered program focuses on four key areas: healthy crewmembers; clean air and surfaces; more space, fewer touchpoints; and travel flexibility. Visit jetblue.com/safety to learn more.
